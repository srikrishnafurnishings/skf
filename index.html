About Us

Crafting Beautiful Interiors Since 2018

Sri Krishna Furnishings is a premier interior design and furnishings company based in Hyderabad, specializing in creating elegant and functional spaces that reflect your personal style. With over a decade of experience, we have transformed countless homes and offices across Telangana. Founded and owned by Mr. Balram, who brings a keen eye for detail and a passion for quality craftsmanship to every project, Sri Krishna Furnishings is built on the belief that great design should be accessible to everyone. That’s why we offer personalized solutions at competitive prices. From luxurious curtains to custom blinds, premium sofas to exquisite wallpapers, we provide end-to-end interior solutions that elevate your living and working spaces.
